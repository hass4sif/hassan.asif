Research

I study how people use phones, laptops, studios, and platforms to record, edit, and circulate devotional media—and how those technical choices become ethical and theological questions.

Dissertation

“Remixing Devotion: Digital Media Practices in Pakistan.”

Focus: devotional remixing, platform circulation, and the politics of visibility.

Methods

Close reading · Ethnography · Media genealogy

Attention to infrastructures, everyday workflows, and “small” technical decisions.

Core interests

Digital religion · Remix aesthetics · AI media · Platforms & policy · South Asian media worlds
